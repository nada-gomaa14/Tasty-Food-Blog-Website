Pizza Bombs


Ingredients Instructions
  • 3 tablespoons butter, for greasing the pan
  • 16 ½ oz can of biscuit dough(465 g), 1 can
  • marinara sauce
  • sliced pepperoni
  • 8 oz ball of mozzarella cheese(225 g), cut into cubes
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • salt to taste
  • pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon italian seasoning
  • shredded parmesan cheese, for topping
  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and grease with butter.
  2. Cut each biscuit in half. Press each half into a circle with your thumb.
  3. Place a small dollop of marinara sauce, 1 pepperoni, and 1 cube of mozzarella cheese in the center of each biscuit round.
  4. Bring the edges up and over, pressing them together and being sure to leave no gaps for the filling to leak out. Lay the pizza bombs on the prepared baking sheet.
  5. Combine the melted butter, garlic, salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning in a small bowl.
  6. Brush the butter mixture onto each pizza bomb and top with Parmesan.
  7. Bake for 15-20 minutes, until the bombs have nicely browned.
  8. Once they are cool enough to handle, serve with marinara sauce alongside.


Peanut Butter Energy Bites


Ingredients Instructions
  • ⅔ cup creamy peanut butter(160 g)
  • 1 cup old-fashioned oats(100 g), plus extra for rolling
  • banana, sliced
  • 1 ½ tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon milk
  • ¼ cup mini chocolate chips(40 g), plus more for rolling
  • ¼ cup flax seeds(40 g), optional
  1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl until well-combined.
  2. Cover with plastic wrap and chill in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Once done chilling, roll into approximately 1 inch (2.5 cm) balls. (Cover hands in a light layer of butter or oil to facilitate rolling and avoid getting sticky hands.)
  4. Lay out a thin layer of oats and chocolate chips on a cutting board or work surface and roll the balls in the mixture. Finish off by rolling each ball between your hands to pack in the oats and chocolate chips.
  5. Enjoy!


3 Ingredient Peanut Butter Cookies


Ingredients Instructions
  • 1 cup peanut butter(240 g)
  • ½ cup sugar(100 g)
  • 1 egg
  1. Preheat oven to 350ºF (180ºC).
  2. In a large bowl, mix together the peanut butter, sugar, and egg.
  3. Scoop out a spoonful of dough and roll it into a ball. Place the cookie balls onto a nonstick baking sheet.
  4. For extra decoration and to make them cook more evenly, flatten the cookie balls by pressing a fork down on top of them, then press it down again at a 90º angle to make a criss-cross pattern.
  5. Bake for 8-10 minutes or until the bottom of the cookies are golden brown.
  6. Remove from baking sheet and cool.
